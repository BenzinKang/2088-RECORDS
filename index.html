The Label

Sound as a signal from the future

2088 Records was founded on a simple premise: the most enduring electronic music treats production as architecture. We work with a small, deliberate roster building techno, ambient and electro that rewards close listening.

From debut EPs to full-length transmissions, every release passes through the same lens — restraint, texture, and a refusal to chase trend cycles.
