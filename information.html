Information

Who We Are

2088 Records is an independent label building electronic music for listeners who treat an album as a place to return to, not a stream to skip through.

Origin

Founded on restraint

2088 Records was established by sensational producer N2V in 2019, aiming to bring audiences into a completely different futuristic world. Seven years on, the label has grown into a small international roster spanning Electronic, Chill and Ambient — but the founding rule hasn't changed: fewer releases, made with more care.

Mission

Build records that outlast the news cycle

We sign artists for the body of work, not the single. Every release is developed over months, mastered for both club systems and headphones, and packaged as a complete object — art, sequencing, and sound treated as one decision.

Mission

Depth over volume

Release fewer records, and give each one the time it needs to be finished properly.

Vision

A label without borders

Build a roster that spans continents and studios, united by craft rather than geography or scene.

Philosophy

Production as architecture

Treat rhythm, space and silence the way an architect treats structure, load and light.
